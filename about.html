About Us

Crow is a newly started garbage management service provider to ensure the proper manages of your daily garbage. Garbage is a clear threat for our environment. We have to manage them recycle them to protect our environment. We provide daily and monthly basis service. We have a system to collect your household garbage by our service van. We take order online for providing service. The charge is of 108 tk. only for monthly service. We provide emergency service also. The charge for emergency service is 20 tk. only for per bag of garbage. You can take our daily, monthly and emergency service according to your need. We have an amazing facility for you. We provide reward points while you taking our service. If you take our daily service you will get 1 reward point. 1 point means 12 tk. You can use this point to pay the bills.
